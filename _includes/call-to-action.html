Welcome to Piramide/Dragonfly of Colorado! We offer a vibrant selection of fair trade clothing, jewelry, and accessories from Bali and Thailand, including our exclusive "Dragonfly of Colorado" line. Our store also features eco-friendly, natural fiber clothing from the USA and around the world. Located at 735 Manitou Avenue in historic Manitou Springs, at the base of Pikes Peak, we provide a stress-free shopping experience with a wide range of styles and sizes at reasonable prices. For every Elephant Pant sold, we donate $1 to the Save Elephant Foundation in Chiang Mai, Thailand.
